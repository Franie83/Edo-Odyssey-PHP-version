## 1. Introduction & Vision

### What is Edo Odyssey?

Edo Odyssey is a **comprehensive digital tourism platform** created by the Edo State Tourism Agency (EDSTA) to:

- **Promote** Edo State's rich cultural heritage and tourism assets.
- **Connect** tourists with certified local guides, hotels, restaurants, and attractions.
- **Streamline** booking, payments, and review processes.
- **Provide** data-driven insights for strategic tourism planning.

### Why This Matters

- Edo State is the **Cradle of Black Civilization** – home to the ancient Benin Kingdom, UNESCO-recognized bronze art, and diverse natural attractions.
- Tourism is a **major economic driver**, and this platform modernizes how tourists discover and experience Edo State.
- The platform positions Edo State as a **leading tourism destination** in Nigeria and Africa.

- **Category Browsing** – Cultural Heritage, Nature & Wildlife, Historical Sites, Arts & Crafts, Religious Sites, Festivals.
- **Featured Attractions** – Top attractions highlighted for promotion.
- **Featured Guides** – Certified tour guides available for booking.
- **Upcoming Events** – Cultural festivals and events with countdown timers.
- **Accommodations** – Hotels and lodging options.
- **Latest News** – Tourism news and updates.

#### 3.2 Attractions

- **Sort** – Sort by popularity, price (low-high / high-low), and highest rated.

#### 3.4 Hotels

- **Reviews & Ratings** – Tourists can rate and review hotels (pending admin approval).

#### 3.5 Restaurants
- **Search & Filter** – Search by name, city, cuisine type, price range, and rating.
- **Sort** – Sort by price (low-high / high-low) and highest rated.
- **Detailed View** – Cuisine, opening hours, average price, and menu.
- **Reviews & Ratings** – Tourists can rate and review restaurants (pending admin approval).

#### 3.6 Events
- **Search & Filter** – Search by name, category, city, price range, and rating.
- **Sort** – Sort by date (earliest / latest), price (low-high / high-low), and highest rated.
- **Upcoming Filter** – Show only upcoming events.
- **Detailed View** – Description, location, date/time, ticket price, and organizer information.
- **Reviews & Ratings** – Tourists can rate and review events (pending admin approval).

#### 3.7 News
- **View Articles** – Browse tourism news, updates, and announcements.
- **Comments** – Authenticated users can comment on news articles.

#### 3.8 Authentication & User Accounts
- **Registration** – Choose role: Tourist, Guide, Hotel, Restaurant, Event Organizer.
- **Login** – Secure authentication with password.
- **Quick Login (Demo)** – One-click login for different roles (Super Admin, Agency Admin, Tourist, Guide, Hotel, Restaurant).

## 5. Booking Workflow

### 5.1 Full Approval Flow
1. **User creates a booking** → Status: `Pending`.
2. **Admin approves the booking** → Status: `AdminApproved` (admin can add a comment).
3. **Vendor (guide/hotel/restaurant/event) accepts or rejects**:
   - Accept → Status: `VendorAccepted` (vendor can add a comment).
   - Reject → Status: `VendorRejected` (vendor can add a reason).
4. **Vendor marks booking as complete** → Status: `Completed` (tourist earns heritage points).
5. **Tourist can cancel** → Status: `Cancelled` (if pending or approved by admin).
6. **Full status history** – Every step is logged with timestamp, user, and comment.

### 5.2 Who Sees What
| User | Can See |
|------|---------|
| **Tourist** | Their own bookings + approval history + vendor responses |
| **Vendor** | Bookings assigned to them + approval history + admin comments |
| **Admin** | All bookings + full approval history + comments |

## 8. Technical Architecture

### 8.1 Stack
| Component | Technology |
|-----------|------------|
| **Backend** | Laravel 12 (PHP 8.2+) |
| **Frontend** | Bootstrap 5, Chart.js, Blade templates |
| **Database** | SQLite (development) / MySQL (production) |
| **QR Code** | chillerlan/php-qrcode |
| **Authentication** | Laravel Breeze (customized) |
| **Deployment** | Docker (containerized) |

### 8.2 Security
- **Role-based access** – Tourist, Guide, Hotel, Restaurant, Event Organizer, Agency Admin, Super Admin.
- **Admin middleware** – Protects admin routes.
- **Ownership checks** – Vendors can only manage their own offerings.
- **CSRF protection** – All forms use CSRF tokens.
- **Input validation** – All user inputs are validated.

### 8.3 Deployment (Docker)
- **One-command setup** – `docker compose up -d --build`
- **Nginx web server** – Serves static files and proxies PHP requests.
- **PHP-FPM** – Processes PHP code.
- **MySQL** – Production database.
- **Volume mapping** – Persistent storage for database and uploaded files.
